Telangana Facts

  • Telangana is the 29th state of India, formed on June 2, 2014.
  • It is known for its rich history, culture, and heritage.
  • The capital city is Hyderabad, which is a major IT hub in India.

Museum Facts

  • Telangana has several famous museums, including the Salar Jung Museum in Hyderabad.
  • The Nizam Museum showcases artifacts from the Nizam era.
  • Warangal Fort Museum houses artifacts from the Kakatiya dynasty.
