Telangana Facts

Telangana is one of the 29 states in India, located in the southern part of the country.
Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is known as the 'City of Pearls' and is a major IT hub.
The state has a rich cultural heritage with classical dance forms like Perini Shivatandavam and festivals like Bonalu.
Telugu is the official language, and the state celebrates its literary heritage with events like the Telangana Sahitya Sabha.
It boasts scenic landscapes with attractions such as the Golconda Fort, Warangal Fort, Nagarjuna Sagar Dam, and Pakhal Lake.
The state is known for architectural marvels like the Ramappa Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
Telangana offers a unique culinary experience with dishes like Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, and Pesarattu.
It is a leader in technology and innovation with initiatives like T-Hub fostering entrepreneurship.
Telangana has rich biodiversity with national parks like Kasu Brahmananda Reddy National Park and Mahavir Harina Vanasthali National Park.
The state celebrates various festivals with great enthusiasm, including Bathukamma and Sankranti.
