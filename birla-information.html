Birla Science Museum Information

Location

The museum is situated near Birla Mandir (Birla Temple) in Hyderabad, offering a picturesque view of the city.

Exhibits

It features various exhibits covering topics such as physics, chemistry, biology, electronics, and astronomy. There are interactive displays and models that make learning about science engaging and accessible.

Planetarium

The museum includes a Birla Planetarium, which hosts shows on astronomy, space exploration, and celestial phenomena. The planetarium is equipped with modern projection technology to simulate the night sky and cosmic events.

Other Facilities

Visitors can also explore the Dinosaurium, which showcases replicas of dinosaurs and fossils, adding an element of paleontology to the museum's offerings.

Visiting Hours

Typically, the museum is open to visitors from morning to evening, though it's advisable to check the current operating hours before planning your visit.
